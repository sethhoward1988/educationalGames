Gamification. Making education fun again.

Most children find filling out worksheets mundane and boring. The learning center targets that notion by creating fun and innovative games that attract children. Whether is be geography, math, science, we're sure to match a game that will interest your child.

Dynamic Content. Get Involved.

The Learning center gives the ability for teachers and parents to create content for their own children. Teachers can create practice spelling lists and record their own voice that their child will then listen to. Teachers and parents can also limit access to games that they find relevant to the material being taught in class.

Analytics. Monitor Progress

Each time your child finishes a game, the results are stored on our secure servers. Teachers and parents can visualize results through charts and graphs and track the students usage and correlations to performance on in-class quizzes and tests.
